## 🧭 Project Overview

Wandr is a group trip planning app that helps friends plan the perfect trip, all in one place.

---

## 🎯 Problem

* Planning group trips is fragmented across chats, spreadsheets, and notes
* Difficult to align on budgets, preferences, and schedules
* Decision-making is slow and often frustrating for the organizer

## 👤 Target User

* Young professionals planning trips with friends
* Small groups (3–8 people) coordinating travel collaboratively

## 💡 Solution

* AI-powered centralized itinerary builder that evolves as more people fill out their travel preferences
* In under 2 minutes, you get a shareable itinerary that reflects each person's input
